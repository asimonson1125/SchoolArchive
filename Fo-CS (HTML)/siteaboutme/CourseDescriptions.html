My Courses

Period 1: Foundationd of Computer Science

In this class we learn the basics of computers, the internet, and coding. We are currently learning HTML and CSS. Later we will look at javascript and problem solving.

Period 2: AP Computer Science A

In this class we learn more advanced coding techniques and Java. We are currently learning about arrays.

Period 3: AP Lang

In this class we go into further depth about how to formulate our writing to make it more effective. Right now we are learning about persuasive writing and warrents.

Period 4: AP Physics 1

In this class we learn about the laws of physics and do calculations about real world scenarios to predict their outcomes. Currently we are learning about harmonic motion and springs.

Period 5: Taking Informed Action

This class is actually We The People, but that technically is not supposed to be all in it's own class. In this class we compete in the We The People cometition by formuating answers to current government issues and relate our reasoning to past issues and the creation of the Constitution and its amendments.

Period 6: Honors Pre-Calculus

In this class we learn about all sorts of mathematical things. Currently we are learning about the law of sines and the case of SSA.
