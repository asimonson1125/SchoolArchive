How I feel about School

5 Things I Like about School

  1. I like engaging with other people
  2. I like the extracirricular opportunities
  3. I like learning in the classes that interest me
  4. I like the sense of structure that having to go to school gives to my day
  5. I like the unity created by everyone hating school

Things I Dislike about School

  1. I dislike the structure and bureaucracy the school enforces
    • and everything about it
  2. I dislike the learning environment
  3. I dislike having to deal with stupid people
  4. I dislike many of the topics forced upon us
    • Particularly English and Foreign Languages
  5. I dislike the universality of the education level
    • It makes nobody special
